League Graphs Alpha

This is League Graphs, a free statistics tracker for League of Legends.

How it works:

Once your summoner name is entered here once, your stats will be tracked automatically forever and ever afterwards. Every player known to the site gets updated at least once every 8 hours. Note: If you play more than 10 games in 8 hours, just come enter your name and your profile will be nommed manually.
