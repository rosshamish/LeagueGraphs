League Graphs

This is League Graphs, a free statistics tracker for League of Legends.

How it works:

Once your summoner name is entered here once, your stats will be tracked automatically forever and ever afterwards. Every player known to the site gets nommed at least once a day.
The auto-tracker is limited to 10 games per day. If you play more, just come enter your name and your profile will be nommed manually.

Enter your summoner name. Be known.
